Data about Gross Weight Of Goods Handled in All Ports in Europe

This dahsboard represents data related to the gross weight of goods handled in all European ports according to the year selected in the drop-down menu. We have, following the order from left to right and from top to bottom, a map indicating the types of goods most handled in the country (the information is available with the mouse), a map indicating the quantity of goods handled by country, a bar chart indicating the quantity of imported and exported goods, a polar chart to visualize the quantity transported of each type of goods, a pie chart to visualize the most important countries in the maritime goods traffic and an evolution of the maritime traffic since the years 2000. All quantities are given in THS_T. The meaning of "GT" codes is available here.
